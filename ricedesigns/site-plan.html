Overview

Purpose

I would like to create a website for my business of making rings. I would like it to be a place where I can advertise my rings and other pieces of my art. What makes my business different than others is the rings I make are unique and made with special materials like opal and gold. Each ring is very customizable to each person. Wouldnt you like a unique and one-of-a-kind ring? One that stands out from all the rest? That is the purpose of my business, to bring exciting new styles of rings to people.

Audience

My target audience are people between the ages of 20 and 80 with more of a focus on people between the ages of 20 and 40. The people I am most likely to have wanting to purchase a ring as a gift are those in the 20-40 and in the 40 and above it is more for people who are wanting a new wedding band. The price of the rings is relatively low at between 75 and 100 USD, allowing me to sell to people in most financial situations unlike most other ring places with their very high prices.

Content

Home page

Advertise Business: The first purpose of my website is to advertise my business. People will come to my website to see the many different rings that I create. This will be a great way for me to display my rings for more people to be able to see. People will be able to discover all of my unique designs that are hand made by me. I will be able to peak people interest from the first page to purchase a ring. The ability to customize their own ring will be a real selling point from the advertisement page of my website. I can use a lot of the different materials that I use in each ring to make people interested in purchasing one of my rings for themselves. I can also put an FAQ on my site so people can ask questions and I can discover more of what people are looking for so that I can adjust my business in whatever ways are needed. There are also a lot of questions people have with jewelry and the materials I would need to answer.

Advertise Business: This page will include a quick link to the shop page so people can easily access the next page. There will be a small product gallery made of three different products. The products will be best sellers, new products, or products that are out for only a limited amount of time. A brief description will also be provided on this page to give people and good idea of what it is exactly this business is. Also on this page will be an FAQ which will be available for people to be able to ask questions pertaining to shipping and fulfillment and more information on the products. Also included on this page will be links to social media pages and a quick reference to contact information will be provided.

Images for the Home page

Shop

Sell Rings: The second purpose of my site is for me to be able to sell my rings. When people come to my site, they should be able to find what the prices of my rings are. They should be able to select their size of ring and the style they want for their ring. There will also be a place for people to go to be able to customize a ring for themselves, they will be able to choose their own materials for the rings to make it more personal to them. The ability to make the ring your own is a very important part of my business. With the unique styles I offer already, having people be able to create their own designs give more uniqueness to my business. The customization makes my site a reason for people to come to my site rather than going to another site of the same thing. With the customization if the rings from my site, I will have a place where people can ask about/request different materials that I dont have listed on my site so that I can add more things that people like to my list of materials and be able to expand the number of costumers that my site receives and increasing my overall business.

Shop: the shop page will include a product gallery for the rest of the products. Under each of the products will include the product name, the price, and whether or not the product is still available. The header and footer will of course remain the same as it is on the home page.

Blog

Blog/Information: Because I hand make each ring and create each of the designs for each ring I make. This is a big reason my site will be one that people will want to go too, the uniqueness of what I make is the reason my website can get a lot of traffic. A blog is a good way to have a more personal connection with my audience. Having a place where I can show the process of making each ring and have other information there for people will help me be able to answer questions they might have. This could also be helpful as a place to talk about the different materials used and what I am coming up with next. This is also a place where I could help other that are interested in making rings themselves to be able to get tips and trick to be able to try making rings for themselves. I can also post videos of me making the rings to spark the interest of people to want to buy/make rings. I can make more personal posts on the blog to make people more interested in my business and what I do.

Blog: The blog page will be a place where I can post news and updates about products and other information or changes that I make. Other information will also be posted about materials used, ring sizing, and shipping information. The header and the footer will remain the same as on other pages.
